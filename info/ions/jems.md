# jems

**jems** are **json-expressed modules**:

```javascript
  ~
  { "re":
        { "id":"jem@doma.in"
        , "is":"a json-expressed module"
        , "by":"mike.lee@iskitz"
        , "at":"2017.11.13-08...2007.09-04"
        , "in":
              [   "san-jose.california.usa.earth"
              , "forest-hills.new-york.usa.earth"
              ]
        }
        
  , "do":
        [ "an aesop action"
        , /a storie action/
        , {"if":"do.times", "<":7, "repeat":"do"}
        ]
    
  , "an aesop action"
  :     "log Hi! #do.times@"
  
  , "a storie action"
  :     /log Hi! #do.times@/
  }
  ;
```
